From The Sea To Your Hearts...

Sustainability is key to life. Here, at Delmar we offer world class seafood processing facilities focusing on sustainability and ecology preservation. Employing environmentally friendly practises, Delmar ensures freshly processed fish products composing of fish fillets are distributed across its brances spanning all over India. The fishing points are considered after careful research while preserving the local ecosystem.

Trust and loyalty are the core fundamentals on which Delmar is built and we strive to maintain good relations with our clientele through mutual understanding and transparency in communication.

Only The Best For The Best


After undergoing stringent cleaning and handling measures and a series of quality assurance checks, the food is processed right on arrival to preserve its taste, richness and quality.

All stages from procuring to processing to packing, our employees work to provide the best of the food from sea to your plates. Banking on new developments and advancements in the field of seafood business, Delmar is always one step ahead of its competitors striving to bring fresh, nutritious and succulent seafood to the platter everywhere.

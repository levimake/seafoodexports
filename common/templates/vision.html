Vision

On a journey to transform seafood business.

Combining inland, marine catches and aquaculture production, Delmar seeks to bring the world to your plate as India's appetite for processed seafood is on the rise. Fish and fish products have been vital to the regional cuisine and serves as an important addition to the staple diet consumed by millions on a daily basis.

Incorporating quality into variety

Delmar exports the most sought after fish categories including Tuna, Pomfrets, Mackerels, Seer fish, Sea bass and other exotic fish types such as Shrimps, Crabs, Octopus and Lobsters.
